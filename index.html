UWSound Map

The UW Sound Map aims to create a soundscape of the university of Washington that gives listeners an auditory experience rather than just a visual experience.

Everyone is invited to contribute to the map with recordings of their own. Please submit recordings to msablan @ uw.edu with the following information:

  • Name of place within UW
  • Date/Time
  • Description of recording

*NOTE: All recordings must meet archival standards of 48kHz sample rate/24-bit depth/wav file
